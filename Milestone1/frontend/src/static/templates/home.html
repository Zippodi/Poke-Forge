Welcome to the Poke-Forge App!

Poke-Forge is a Pokemon Team Building App. It allows you to create many teams of 6 pokemon, so that you can have a place where you can easily store and manage your teams to use in whatever pokemon battles you want. Just like in the games, each pokemon can hold an item, as well as having their own list of moves. You can also give each of your teams a name, so that you can easily remember which team is which, you also can view your teams stats: what types you have on your team, as well as what types they are effective/weak against. When creating a team you can also choose whether or not you want to make your team public to other users.
